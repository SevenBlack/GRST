Core Competences

Battery Safety Technology

All energy storage systems have a risk of unexpected environmental conditions or defaults that can create an accidental or uncontrolled energy release.

We put great effort to improve the overall battery safety to a higher level, through battery materials modifications and structural design.

-- GRST’s proprietary modified NMC cathode materials has better thermal stability

-- GRST battery uses ceramic coated separator which keeps stable at up to 250 °C, comparing with the 120-140°C melting temperature of conventional PP(polypropylene) and PE(polyethylene) separators.

GRST battery keeps inert in a nail penetration test.



GRST lithium-ion battery shows excellent stability in the nail penetration test, even in the overcharging state (SoC: 110%), no fire or explosion.


Green Manufacturing Technology

Conventional battery manufacturing process uses toxic organic solvents such as N-methyl-pyrrolidone (NMP) and expensive fluorinated polymers such as polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) for the electrode coating.

GRST applies a full water-based green production process by using water and water soluble and florine-free binder system in the battery electrodes preparation to achieve zero toxic organic solvent emissions and lower productions costs.

Conventional Non-Green Manufacturing Process



  • Toxic organic solvent (N-methyl-pyrrolidone (NMP)) and expensive and non-green fluorinated polymers (polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF)).
  • Higher capital expenditure, and production, material and regulatory costs.
  • Higher energy consumption in electrode production and solvent recovery processes.
  • Higher risk of future liabilities.

GRST Green Manufacturing Process

  • Water as a solvent and water-soluble fluorine-free polymer as a binding agent.
  • Lower capital expenditure, and operational and material costs.
  • Lower energy consumption for electrode drying and high throughput.
  • Complements customer and partners’ vision of green supply chain.

GRST concerns the potential environmental impact of lithium ion battery disposal, and willing to take its responsibilities to recycle the power battery as well as recovery the valuable resources.

By using water soluble binder and water to prepare the battery electrodes, our battery can be recycled in a more environmental-friendly way by using water to extract electrode active materials from metallic substrate.

The electrode materials in GRST lithium-ion battery will be recycled, regenerated and reused as battery raw materials.
